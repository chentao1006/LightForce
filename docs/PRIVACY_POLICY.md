# Privacy Policy for Light Force

**Last Updated:** March 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Light Force ("the Extension"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our Extension operates regarding your data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect any personal data.** 

Light Force is designed to work entirely locally within your Chrome browser. The Extension does not collect, track, transmit, or store any personal information, browsing history, or user data. 

*   **Website Content:** To fulfill its single purpose of changing dark themes to light themes, the Extension injects small pieces of CSS via local script (`content.js`). It modifies the visual appearance of the website you are currently visiting directly on your device. It does not read, copy, or monitor the actual content, passwords, or any data entered on those websites.
*   **Storage Permission:** The Extension requests the `storage` permission solely to save your preference of whether the extension is toggled "ON" or "OFF". This single boolean value is stored locally on your own device using Chrome's built-in sync storage and is never sent to any external server.
*   **ActiveTab Permission:** The Extension requests the `activeTab` permission to briefly interact with the tab you currently have open only when you explicitly interact with the Extension's popup toggle, allowing it to instantly reload the page to apply your chosen theme.

## 2. Third-Party Access

Because we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or disclose any information to third parties. There are no analytics trackers, no telemetry scripts, and no external API calls within this Extension.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any significant changes occur that alter our stance on data collection (which we do not foresee), we will notify you through an update to the Extension.

## 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concern regarding this Privacy Policy or the operational practices of Light Force, please feel free to reach out.
