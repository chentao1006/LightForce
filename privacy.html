Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. No Data Collection

Light Force does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data. Everything happens locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The extension requires permissions only to modify the CSS of the websites you visit to apply the light theme. This is necessary for the core functionality and is executed entirely within your browser.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising networks.

4. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
